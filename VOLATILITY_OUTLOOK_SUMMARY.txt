TASK 2: CANONICAL REFERENCE MAPPING
================================================================================

Defined in rollups.py (_aggregate_volatility_by_asset_class):

  EQUITIES  → SPX      (S&P 500 index)
  FX        → DXY      (US Dollar Index)
  RATES     → US10Y    (10-Year Treasury Yield)
  COMMODITIES → CL     (Crude Oil - most liquid)
  METALS    → GC       (Gold)
  ENERGY    → CL       (Crude Oil)
  CRYPTO    → BTC      (Bitcoin)
  VOLATILITY → VIX     (VIX Index)
  GENERAL   → None     (Too broad for single instrument)
  CREDIT    → None     (Too broad for single instrument)

FX Reference Choice: DXY
  Rationale:
    - Most common vol surface anchor in macro research
    - Represents basket of major currencies (EUR, JPY, GBP, CAD, SEK, CHF)
    - Inverse relationship with EURUSD is well-understood
    - Deterministic source allows confident canonical assignment
